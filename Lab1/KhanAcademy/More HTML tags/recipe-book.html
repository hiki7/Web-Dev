Veggie Garlic Noodles

  • Time: 2 hours
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
vegetable oil 2
cloves garlic, minced 5
green onions, sliced, divided 4
carrots, cut into matchsticks 2
cup snap peas 1
tablespoons brown sugar 2
tablespoons soy sauce 3
oz dried rice noodles, cooked 9

Step 1: Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the garlic and 3 green onions and cook for 2–3 minutes, until fragrant and tender. Add the carrots and snap peas, then stir in the brown sugar and soy sauce.
Step 2: Add the noodles and toss for a couple of minutes, until the noodles are well-coated and the vegetables are tender. Season with salt and pepper.
Step 3: Serve immediately, garnished with the remaining green onion.
Step 4: Enjoy!

Bacon And Egg Ramen

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
package instant ramen 1
tablespoon unsalted butter 1
fried egg 1
slices bacon, cooked and chopped 3
tablespoon thinly sliced scallions 1

Step 1: In a small pot, bring 2 cups (480 ml) of water to a boil. Add the ramen seasoning packet and a tablespoon of butter. Stir until the butter melts and then add the ramen noodles and cook until tender, 2 minutes. Pour the ramen into a bowl.
Step 2: Top the ramen with the fried egg, bacon, and scallions and season with salt and pepper.
Step 3: Enjoy!

Weekday Meal-Prep Pesto Chicken & Veggies

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
tablespoons olive oil 2
boneless, skinless chicken thighs 4
green beans 1 lb
cups cherry tomatoes, halved 2
cup basil pesto ½

Step 1: Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat, then add the chicken thighs. Season with salt and pepper. Cook until the chicken is no longer pink in the center, then remove from the pan. Slice into strips.
Step 2: Add the green beans to the same pan and cook until tender-crisp.
Step 3: Return the chicken strips to the pan, then add the tomatoes and pesto. Stir until fully incorporated.
Step 4: Serve immediately or divide into 4 food storage containers and store in the refrigerator for up to 4 days.
Step 5: Enjoy!
